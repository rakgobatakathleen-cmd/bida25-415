More Than Just A Delta

Botswana is celebrated for the breathtaking waterways of the Okavango Delta, but a wealth of diverse landscapes awaits the intrepid traveler. "Delta and Beyond" is your compass to unearthing the secluded corners of this incredible country.

From the sweeping salt pans of Makgadikgadi to the dense, elephant-rich woodlands of Chobe, we invite you to experience a sustainable, low-impact, and high-value journey. Uncover deeply rooted San cultural encounters and empower your travel story with confidence.
